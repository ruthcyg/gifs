❤️ Qualities of Love ❤️ 1 Corinthians 13:4-7 ❤️

💖 Patient – Love is willing to wait without frustration.
💞 Kind – Love is gentle and caring.
💚 Not Envious – Love does not get jealous of others.
✨ Does Not Boast – Love is humble and does not brag.
💫 Not Proud – Love is not arrogant or self-centered.
🙏 Does Not Dishonor Others – Love is respectful and doesn’t bring shame or harm.
🤲 Not Self-Seeking – Love is selfless and considers others’ needs.
😌 Not Easily Angered – Love is calm and slow to anger.
💦 Keeps No Record of Wrongs – Love forgives and doesn’t hold onto past hurts.
😇 Does Not Delight in Evil – Love does not take pleasure in wrongdoing.
🌟 Rejoices with the Truth – Love celebrates honesty and integrity.
🛡️ Always Protects – Love provides a sense of safety and support.
🤝 Always Trusts – Love has confidence and faith in others.
🌈 Always Hopes – Love remains optimistic and looks forward with hope.
🌱 Always Perseveres – Love endures, even in tough times.
