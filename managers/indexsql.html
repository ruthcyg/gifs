Why Use Raw SQL?

© Ruth Ewho
Custom Queries ▼
If the ORM doesn’t cover your specific need, raw SQL gives you full control over the database query, allowing you to write exactly what you need.
Optimizing Performance ▼
Raw SQL can sometimes be faster, especially for highly optimized queries where the ORM might add unnecessary overhead.
Complex Aggregations or Data Transformations ▼
When dealing with big data or advanced analytics, raw SQL may help you go beyond ORM capabilities, providing a way to perform complex aggregations or transformations.
