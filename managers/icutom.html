QuerySet.as_manager()

Creates a Manager that includes all public methods of a QuerySet.

Rules for Method Copying

Public methods are copied to the Manager; private ones aren't unless opted in.

Handling Private Methods

Private methods (with underscore) are excluded unless specifically opted in.

Using queryset_only

Setting queryset_only=True keeps methods exclusive to the QuerySet.
