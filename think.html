Stop Trying to Read Minds
“For as he thinketh in his heart, so is he.” — Proverbs 23:7
In a world where we’re constantly trying to decode people’s thoughts, we overlook one important truth: actions reveal the real story. People may use words to paint a picture, but their true character is reflected in how they behave. When we focus on observing actions instead of analyzing every word, we allow the truth to speak for itself.
