Subqueries Within Annotations for Complex Data Retrieval

Purpose: Annotations can include subqueries to pull in related data, enabling complex calculations and data retrieval within a single query.
Use Case: Annotate each Product with its latest sale price from a related Sale model, avoiding additional joins or multiple queries.
Explanation: The subquery fetches the latest sale price by ordering sales in descending order and retrieving the top record. This approach is powerful for fetching recent data or related records without additional joins.
