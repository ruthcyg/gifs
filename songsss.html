A Song for You, For Us 🎶

💪 To the men who carry burdens unseen,

✨ Pushing through each day, chasing dreams,

🛠️ You work, you strive, you do your part,

❤️ But don’t forget to care for your heart.

🔥 The world asks you to stand strong, to fight,

🌙 To keep the fire burning, through day and night,

👤 But you’re not just a role, not just a name,

🙌 You’re a person too—don’t feel the shame.

🌺 And to the women, the quiet warriors, too,

⚖️ Balancing love and work, always staying true.

💖 You nurture, you lead, you give all you’ve got,

✨ But remember, your worth isn’t tied to the lot.

💫 The world may tell you to bend and break,

🕊️ To put everyone else first, for their sake,

🎤 But your dreams, your voice, they matter too,

🚫 Never let anyone silence you.

🤝 Together, we rise, through struggles and strife,

🏗️ Chasing our dreams, building our life.

💑 Men and women, we all share the fight,

🌍 For love, for peace, for what’s right.

💨 So, take a moment, step back and breathe,

🌅 You’re not alone in this journey, you see.

🔥 No matter the work, the grind, the fight,

💫 Your value is more than what you give tonight.

🌠 For we are people, with hopes and dreams,

🌈 Not just roles or tasks or what it seems.

💖 Let’s honor ourselves, our worth, our grace,

🌟 And embrace the love that lights our space.
